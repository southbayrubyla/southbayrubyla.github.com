About

Welcome to the SouthBay Ruby LA study group website. We are a group of developers, designers, and Ruby on Rails enthusiasts dedicated to studying the art of Ruby on Rails development and design. We are an offshoot of the main Ruby LA group in Pasedena and comprise of members from the SouthBay region, but also Long Beach and parts of Orange County. We meet regularly once a week to discuss readings, practice coding, and to plan/do projects.
